A guide on installing MOON into your game!

<Note>
  This product requires 'loadstrings' to be enabled to work.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Aquire MOON">
    Head to our roblox hub and buy MOON. Then you will get a direct message from **Vault** with the download file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the file">
    Download the file from **Vault** and open roblox studio then drop the `.rbxm` file into workspace and ungroup the folders the the correct spaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adding a webhook">
    To allow **MOON** to work you will need a webhook url for your mod calls to log to a discord channel.

    1. Open the **MoonSettings** module that is found under the Server script in the MOON ServerScriptService.
    2. You will see `["WebhookUrl"] = "???"` and you need to paste the webhook url there.

    If you don't know how to get a webhook url follow this video → [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fKksxz2Gdnc](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fKksxz2Gdnc)

    <Danger>
      NEVER SHARE YOUR WEBHOOK URL TO SOMEONE YOU DON'T TRUST.
    </Danger>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Make sure all folders are in the respective places otherwise MOON won’t work.
</Warning>
