A guide on installing MOON into your game!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Aquire STARR">
    Head to our roblox hub and buy MOON. Then you will get a direct message from **Vault** with the download file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the file">
    Download the file from **Vault** and open roblox studio then drop the `.rbxm` file into workspace and ungroup the folders the the correct spaces.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Make sure all folders are in the respective places otherwise MOON won’t work.
</Warning>
